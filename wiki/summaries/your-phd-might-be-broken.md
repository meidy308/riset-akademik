## Key Points
- PhD burnout and "broken" feeling are often caused by **drift** (misalignment in the project's spine) rather than a lack of competence or effort.
- A strong PhD project requires a perfectly aligned "**spine**" consisting of five interconnected components.
- Common causes of drift include supervisor feedback pulling in new directions, "shiny object syndrome," and expanding project scope.
